# SoloSell ERP – Excel Mini ERP for Solo Online Sellers

SoloSell ERP is a lightweight Excel-based mini ERP designed for solo online sellers and micro‑entrepreneurs. It centralizes inventory, sales, and expenses into a dynamic dashboard powered by KPIs, PivotTables, and automated analysis. The system is simple, fast, and fully customizable for small digital businesses.

## Features
- Inventory tracking with profit calculation
- Sales logging with platform and customer details
- Expense management by category
- Interactive dashboard with KPIs and PivotCharts
- Timeline and slicers for dynamic filtering
- Clean, modular structure for easy customization

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard includes:
- Total Sales, Purchases, Expenses, Profit, Unit Sold and Avg Ticket Price
- Sales by SKU (bar chart)
- Profit by product (bar chart)
- Expenses by category (bar or pie chart)
- Revenue over time (line chart)

## How to Use
1. Enter your dates in the **Entry** sheet, with the macros for Purchases and Sales.
2. Log operational expenses in the **Expenses** sheet.
3. Refresh all PivotTables to update the dashboard.
4. Use slicers and the timeline to filter by date, SKU, platform, or category.
